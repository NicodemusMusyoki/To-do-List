Early Pregnancy Awareness

Early pregnancy can have serious health, social, and economic consequences. Understanding its causes and effects can help in prevention.

Causes

  • Lack of proper sex education
  • Peer pressure
  • Sexual abuse
  • Limited access to contraceptives
